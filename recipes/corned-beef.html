Corned Beef Hash

Description:

This corned beef hash is the best way to make any leftover corned beef and cabbage exciting again â€“ but for breakfast!

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 cups diced cooked corned beef
  • 2 cups diced cooked potatoes
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • Parsley

Steps:

  1. In a large cast-iron skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat and add the onions and bell pepper. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes, until the onion is translucent and softened.
  2. Add the corned beef, and potatoes, and stir everything together. Then use a spatula to press down and flatten the ingredients in the pan. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes without stirring so that the bottom gets crispy and browned.
  3. Use a spatula to flip the hash over and cook the other side for another couple of minutes. Stir in the parsley, salt, and pepper.
